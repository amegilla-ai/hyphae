# Hyphae — 5 Whys
## Root Cause Analysis

The 5 Whys establishes the root problem Hyphae exists to solve. Each answer becomes the next question.

---

**Problem statement:** Neurodiverse people experience disproportionate loneliness and social isolation.

---

**Why do neurodiverse people experience disproportionate loneliness and social isolation?**

Because they struggle to maintain relationships consistently over time.

---

**Why do they struggle to maintain relationships consistently over time?**

Because maintaining relationships requires executive functions — time awareness, initiation, working memory, and social tracking — that work differently in neurodiverse brains.

---

**Why does it matter that those functions work differently?**

Because without those functions working reliably, people drift from relationships they value without intending to — not because they care less, but because the internal automatic system that neurotypical people rely on unconsciously simply doesn't operate the same way.

---

**Why can't neurodiverse people close that gap through existing means?**

Because there is no external substitute for those internal functions in the context of personal relationships. Every tool that exists — social media, calendars, contacts apps — was built assuming those functions are present. None of them substitute for the social tracking, initiation prompting, and cadence maintenance that neurodiverse brains don't do automatically.

---

**Why does that gap need to be addressed?**

Because social connection is one of the strongest predictors of health, wellbeing, and longevity. Its absence carries health risks comparable to smoking 15 cigarettes a day. Neurodiverse people — who already face disproportionate rates of anxiety, depression, and poor life outcomes — deserve the same access to the quality of social connection that sustains a good life.

---

## Root Cause

**Neurodiverse brains don't automatically do what neurotypical brains do when it comes to social maintenance — and nothing exists to substitute for that.**

---

## What Hyphae Does

Hyphae externalises the social maintenance system that neurodiverse brains don't run automatically. It is not a social skills tool, a therapy, or a social network. It is external scaffolding — a prosthetic for the executive functions that relationships depend on — so that neurodiverse people can show up consistently for the people they care about, and build the quality of relationships that make a life feel full.

---

*Hyphae — improving the quality of life of neurodiverse people through better relationships.*
